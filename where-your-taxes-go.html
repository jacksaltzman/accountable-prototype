Why is this highlighted differently?

Interest on the debt is highlighted in coral because it represents a mandatory spending obligation that does not fund current government services. Unlike other categories, these dollars go to bondholders‚Äîindividuals, institutions, foreign governments, and the Federal Reserve‚Äîas payment for past borrowing. The size of this category depends on both the total debt and prevailing interest rates.
