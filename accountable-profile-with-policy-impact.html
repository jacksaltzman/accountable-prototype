How we calculate policy impacts

Impact figures are calculated by comparing your tax position under current law to what it would have been under prior law, using your actual filed returns as the baseline. Some factors to consider:

  • Confirmed impacts are calculated directly from changes visible in your filed returns (e.g., a credit that didn't exist before).
  • Estimated impacts involve counterfactual modeling‚Äîwhat your taxes might have been under different rules. These carry more uncertainty.
  • We do not account for behavioral changes you might have made in response to tax policy (e.g., choosing to itemize vs. take the standard deduction).
  • State policy impacts are limited to your state of residence during the relevant tax years.
